    Freedom Convoy of Truckers

    The peacful protest of truckers passing through Canada have stormed the news. Truckers all over Canada and the Western United States are protesting the requierment of vaccines. They are telling their leaders to give them their freedom back. It is reported that there is oround 100,000 trucks that have joined the convoy. The trucks have come to a crawl and blocked off everything across many cities. They don't plan on leaving until the Canadians and Canada itself has been given their freedom back. If you would like to read the storie and go into more detail click on this link to go see what is going on."https://www.foxnews.com/world/truckers-in-canada-explain-the-freedom-convoy-give-people-their-freedom-back"

    Face Mask Rules and Covid End in England

    Prime Minister of England has declared that Covid-19 has changed from a pandemic to an endemic. He said to the public,"this should not be seen as the "finish line" because the virus and future variants cannot be eradicated - instead "we must learn to live with Covid in the same way we live with flu.""
